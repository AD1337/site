About me

Hi, I'm Lucas. I'm an indie game dev from Brazil. I used to be a history teacher, but I started making games in my spare time and really enjoyed it, eventually turning my hobby into my full time job.

I'm interested in history, art, narrative (especially emergent narrative), psychology, philosophy, meditation and many other things.
